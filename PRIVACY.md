## 3. iCloud Sync

This app uses Apple's iCloud service to sync your preferences (such as favorites and reading progress) across your devices. This data:

- Is stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple's privacy policies
- Is not accessible to the developer
- Is managed entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure

All app content is stored locally on your device. Reading progress and favorites are stored locally and optionally synced via iCloud. The developer does not operate any external servers and has no access to any data stored on your device or iCloud account.

## 5. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks. The only external service used is Apple's iCloud, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy and terms of service.

## 6. Children's Privacy

This app is rated 4+ and does not collect personal information from any user, including children under the age of 13. The app contains only educational content and is safe for all ages.
